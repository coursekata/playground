2. What CourseKata Playground Is

Playground is a browser-based interactive coding environment that allows users to write, run, and share code (including R and Python) directly in a web browser, without installing any software on their device.

Technical architecture — what the Product can and cannot do:

Playground runs entirely within your web browser using WebAssembly (WASM) technology. This means:

  • No installation. Nothing is installed on your computer, tablet, or device.

  • No file system access. Playground cannot read, write, modify, or delete files on your device’s file system unless you explicitly grant permission through your browser’s File System Access interface. Playground does not access your operating system, other applications, or documents stored outside the browser.

  • Browser storage. Like many web applications, Playground uses browser storage (specifically IndexedDB and the browser cache) to save your notebooks, settings, and application files between sessions. This storage accumulates over time with regular use and may become significant in size. It is sandboxed to your browser and this website — it cannot be read by other websites or applications, and it is fully isolated from your file system. You can view, manage, and clear this storage at any time through your browser’s settings (typically under Settings → Privacy → Site Data, or the equivalent in your browser). Your browser enforces limits on how much storage any single website may use and may prompt you if those limits are approached. CourseKata has no access to data stored in your browser and cannot recover it if you clear your browser storage.

  • No background processes. Playground does not run any processes on your device outside of your browser tab. Closing the tab ends all activity.

  • Browser sandbox. All code execution occurs within your browser’s security sandbox, which isolates Playground from your operating system and other applications.

  • Network access. Playground may make network requests consistent with normal browser behavior and the same-origin policy. It does not make unauthorized connections to external services.

Because of this architecture, Playground’s ability to affect software or data outside your browser is extremely limited by design. Any claim that Playground caused damage to your device, operating system, or other software should be evaluated in light of these technical constraints.
