Privacy Policy

How CourseKata Playground handles information in the browser-based coding environment.

Summary: CourseKata Playground does not use accounts, analytics, error tracking, or LMS integrations, and does not store your notebooks on any server. The notebooks and code you create are stored locally in your browser and are never sent to CourseKata. Basic technical request information (such as your IP address) is processed by our hosting provider only to deliver and secure the website.

Last Updated: May 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how CourseKata (“we,” “our,” or “us”) handles information in connection with your use of CourseKata Playground (“Playground”).

1. Overview

Playground is a browser-based coding environment that runs entirely on your device. The code, notebooks, and data you create in Playground are stored locally in your browser and are not transmitted to CourseKata.

If you open a notebook from a shared web link, your browser downloads that notebook directly from the location in the link — typically a public site such as GitHub. That request goes to the third-party location you chose, not to CourseKata.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

CourseKata does not collect, store, or process the code, notebooks, or data that you create in Playground. This content is stored locally in your browser using browser storage such as IndexedDB and is not accessible to CourseKata.

Playground does not currently use user accounts, learning management system integrations, analytics tools, or error-tracking services.

3. Local Browser Storage

Playground uses your browser’s local storage, including IndexedDB and cache, to store notebooks, settings, and application data. This data:

  • remains on your device;
  • is controlled by your browser;
  • can be viewed, managed, or deleted through your browser settings; and
  • cannot be accessed or recovered by CourseKata if it is deleted.

Your browser may automatically clear site data in some circumstances, such as when storage limits are reached or when you clear browsing data.

4. Information That May Be Collected Automatically

When you access Playground, our hosting infrastructure may automatically process limited technical information needed to deliver, maintain, and secure the website, such as:

  • IP address;
  • browser type and version;
  • date and time of access;
  • requested URLs and basic request metadata; and
  • technical information used to operate and protect the service.

CourseKata does not use this information to identify individual users and does not combine it with other data to do so.

5. Third-Party Services

Playground is delivered through third-party hosting infrastructure, which may process technical request data as described above in order to provide hosting, delivery, security, and reliability services.

If additional services are added in the future, such as analytics, authentication, error tracking, cloud storage, or learning management system integrations, this Privacy Policy will be updated to reflect those changes before such services are deployed.

6. Children’s Privacy

Playground is designed for students and educators, including in K-12 classrooms. Playground does not collect personal information from any user, including children. Because everything you create stays in your browser and no personal information is sent to or stored by CourseKata, Playground does not collect personal information from children of any age.

7. Data Security

Because Playground runs locally in your browser, the security and persistence of your notebooks and code depend in part on your device, browser, and browser settings. You are responsible for managing your browser storage and protecting access to your device.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the “Last Updated” date above. If we make material changes, we may provide additional notice through the Playground website or related CourseKata communications.
